Protect your brand. Protect your workers

The cultural, geographical, and language divides that occur across complex global supply chains means the labor conditions you want your business to uphold are not always translated to reality, and abuses are easily hidden with one-off audits. Made Ethical, created by [the Build Collective], provides real-time, secure, verified labor conditions to help protect workers and your brand.

Verifiable labor hours

We provide apps to accurately track the hours a worker spends at a factory location, making it easy to resolve wage theft issues and other forms of nonpayment.

Fair Pay Distribution

Workers deserve living wages for their verified hours. We integrate with popular local electronic payment services to quickly facilitate any back-pay and pay differential payments (ensuring this money goes directly to the workers).

Real-time monitoring

Selected workers within a factory are hired and trained to monitor labor conditions real-time, and to quickly verify abuse reports. Workers are also given rights training and encrypted, secure apps to report any work issues they encounter.

Worker-defined standards

A coalition of worker reps, labor groups and legal specialists define work place standards that are used to monitor work conditions, conduct audits, and the actions taken to ensure compliance.

Our system works on multiple devices

Our system's apps are available for Android, iPhone, iOS, and Windows.
