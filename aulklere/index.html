Services

Software

Our software makes it easy to create transparency and oversight in complex supply chains. With our system there is a mobile app for workers to anonymously and safely report working conditions, you can find pre-defined audit forms that suit your business needs, have auditors submit from mobile devices, run reports and assessment scores, and much more.

Certification

With the Aulklere certificate on your labels, your customers can easily make ethical purchases they trust, providing you with a market edge. Our certificate is built on existing practices, put in place by organizations you trust...we did the legwork so you don't have to. Our certificate is based on in-site audits so the standards are verified (not self-reported).

Consulting

Auklere consultants help you develop a clear path to achieve your business' ethical goals, and to ensure the oversight we provide meets your requirements. Our consultants work with leading standards boards, watchdog agencies and NGOs to ensure our auditing standards go beyond compliance and achieve the ethical standards recommended by the organizations your trust.

About Us
